The worldâ€™s only consulting detective, Sherlock Holmes, who was played by Benedict Cumberbatch (Star Trek Into Darkness, Dr. Strange), teams up with his long-suffering, faithful friend, Dr. John Watson, played by Martin Freeman (The Hobbit, Fargo), to solve a dizzying array of crimes and battle diabolical villains with his signature deductive reasoning and his stunning lack of social grace. Co-created by Steven Moffat (Doctor Who, Coupling) and Mark Gatiss (Doctor Who, The League of Gentlemen), Sherlock also stars Amanda Abbington (Mr. Selfridge), Rupert Graves (A Room with a View), and Andrew Scott (Spectre).

Main Characters

Sherlock Holmes: (genius, arrogant, lacking people skills) brought to life by Benedict Cumberbatch
Dr. John H. Watson: (former army doctor recently back from Afghanistan) brought to life by Martin Freeman

Molly Hooper: played by Louise Brealey
Mrs. Hudson: superbly played by Una Stubbs
Inspector Greg Lestrade: played by Rupert Graves

James Moriarty: played by Andrew Scott with a psychotic twist
Irene Adler: played with and without clothes by Lara Pulver
Mary Watson (nee Morstan): played by Amanda Abbington
Mr. & Mrs. Holmes: played by Wanda Ventham and Timothy Carlton
